Install

1) Upload inc/languages/english/onlinetoday.lang.php to your inc/languages/english.

2) Upload inc/plugins/onlinetoday.php to your inc/plugins folder.

3) Go and activate the plug-in in your admin CP -> Plugin Manager -> Show the users that has been online today

Enjoy.

 

Info

This will show up users who has been online today on your page under your "Who's online" part on your index.
